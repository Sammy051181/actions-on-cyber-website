How to upload:
1. Unzip this folder.
2. Go to Cloudflare → Workers & Pages.
3. Open your project.
4. Choose New deployment.
5. Upload the unzipped folder.
6. Deploy.
7. Test https://actionsoncyber.com.

Important:
- Upload the folder that contains index.html, assets, pages and templates.
- Do not change Domains & Routes; those already work.
- Review Privacy and Terms before public promotion.
