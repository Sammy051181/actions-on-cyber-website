About Actions On Cyber

Practical cybersecurity guidance for organisations that cannot afford a dedicated cyber security team.

Why this exists

Many small organisations face real cyber risks but do not have a cyber security department, a CISO or an enterprise-level budget. Actions On Cyber gives those organisations clear, practical steps they can use immediately.

The aim is simple: make useful cyber guidance accessible to the organisations that are often most exposed but least supported.

Owner profile

Actions On Cyber is owned and led by an ex-military cyber security professional with an MSc in Cyber Security, CISSP, CCSP, a BSc in Engineering Management and a Postgraduate Diploma in Business Administration.

This background brings together operational discipline, leadership experience, cyber security knowledge and business understanding. The result is guidance that focuses on what people should actually do when cyber risk becomes real.

What makes it different

  • Military-inspired response drills
  • Plain-English guidance for non-technical teams
  • Free templates, policies and checklists
  • Practical training and consultancy for implementation support
  • A focus on small organisations, charities and community groups that cannot afford enterprise-level cyber support
