What is inside?

Free starter pack contents

The free pack is designed to help small organisations take their first practical cyber steps without being overwhelmed by a large policy library.

Basic cyber governance

A simple governance policy to define who is responsible for cyber security, how often it should be reviewed and what staff are expected to do.

Accounts, passwords and MFA

Simple rules for passwords, multi-factor authentication, shared accounts, starters, leavers and protecting important online services.

Assets and backups

A simple asset register and backup checklist to help track devices, tills, laptops, phones, cloud tools and important business data.

Phishing and scams

A checklist to help staff spot suspicious emails, messages, links, invoices and payment-change requests.

Incident reporting

A simple incident reporting form so staff can record what happened, when it happened, who was affected and what action was taken.

10-point cyber checklist

A plain-English checklist covering backups, updates, MFA, access, phishing, devices, websites and basic incident response.
