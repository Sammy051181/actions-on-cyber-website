Daily Int Brief

Plain-English vulnerability intelligence for small organisations, charities and community groups. We only publish when there is something worth acting on.

Briefing format

  1. What is being reported: the vulnerability or issue causing concern.
  2. What this means: a plain-English explanation without jargon.
  3. Could this affect a small business: who is likely to care and who probably does not.
  4. What to do now: practical checks and actions.
  5. Ask your IT provider: one copy-and-paste question.
  6. Bottom line: the simple takeaway.
