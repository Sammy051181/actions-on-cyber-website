Privacy Policy

How Actions On Cyber handles information when you contact us, use our website, download templates, request training or enquire about consultancy.

Who we are

Actions On Cyber provides practical cyber security guidance, templates, training and consultancy for small organisations without a dedicated security team.

What information we collect

If you contact us, we may collect your name, email address, organisation name, role, enquiry details and any information you choose to include in your message.

If you download resources, request training, register interest in templates or ask about consultancy, we may use the information you provide to respond to your request and manage the service you asked about.

How we use information

We use information to respond to enquiries, provide requested resources, manage training or consultancy requests, improve our website and communicate with you about Actions On Cyber services where appropriate.

Analytics

This website may use analytics tools to understand how visitors use the site, which pages are viewed and how the website can be improved. Analytics information is generally used in an aggregated way and is not intended to identify individual visitors.

Email and contact forms

When you email Actions On Cyber, your message and email address will be used to respond to your enquiry. We do not sell your personal information.

Sharing information

We do not sell personal information. We may share information where necessary to provide a requested service, comply with legal obligations, protect our rights, or use trusted service providers that support the operation of the website and business.

How long we keep information

We keep information only for as long as reasonably necessary for the purpose it was provided, including responding to enquiries, managing services, maintaining records and meeting legal or business requirements.

Your rights

You may ask what personal information we hold about you, request corrections, or ask for information to be deleted where applicable. Some information may need to be retained where required for legal, contractual or legitimate business reasons.

Security

We take reasonable steps to protect information, but no website, email system or online service can be guaranteed to be completely secure.

Contact

For privacy questions, email: hello@actionsoncyber.com.

Important: This privacy policy is a practical starting point and should be reviewed against your actual website setup, analytics tools, email handling, downloads, cookies and business processes before relying on it formally.
