Course Approach

Training built around real actions, not jargon.

Actions On Cyber training is designed to help people recognise common cyber risks and know what to do next. The focus is on simple decisions, clear escalation and practical behaviours that reduce risk.

Discuss training

Training can cover:

Phishing and scams

How to spot suspicious emails, links, attachments, QR codes and payment requests.

Passwords and MFA

Why accounts are targeted and how strong authentication reduces the risk of compromise.

Incident reporting

What staff should report, when to report it and why early reporting matters.

Safe use of AI tools

How to avoid sharing confidential, client or sensitive information with AI tools.
