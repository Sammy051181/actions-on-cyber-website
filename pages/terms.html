Actions On Cyber provides defensive cyber security guidance. The website does not provide offensive hacking instruction, exploit walkthroughs, malware guidance or instructions intended to compromise systems.

Templates, checklists, briefings and guidance are practical starting points and do not constitute legal advice. Organisations should adapt materials to their own context and seek specialist legal, regulatory or professional advice where required.

No guarantee

Cyber security risk cannot be eliminated. The guidance on this site is intended to help organisations improve readiness, reduce common risks and respond more effectively to cyber incidents.

Templates are provided as general examples and starting points. They may need to be changed to reflect your organisation, sector, systems, suppliers, contractual requirements, insurance conditions and legal obligations.
