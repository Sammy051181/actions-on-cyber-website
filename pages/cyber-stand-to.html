Cyber Stand-To

A 10-minute weekly readiness check for organisations that need cyber basics without a full security team.

Weekly readiness check

  1. Check MFA is enabled on email accounts.
  2. Confirm backups completed successfully.
  3. Review who has admin access.
  4. Check leavers no longer have access.
  5. Remind staff about the current scam of the week.
  6. Check the incident contact list is correct.
  7. Record one action for the week.
Battle rhythm

Make readiness routine.

Cyber Stand-To is designed to be quick, repeatable and non-technical. The aim is not perfection. The aim is fewer surprises.

Suggested routine

How to use Cyber Stand-To

Run this once a week with the business owner, office manager, IT provider or whoever looks after systems and staff access.

1. Pick a fixed time

Choose the same 10-minute slot every week so cyber readiness becomes part of normal business rhythm.

2. Record one action

Do not try to fix everything at once. Capture one practical improvement and assign an owner.

3. Follow up next week

Start each Stand-To by checking whether last week's action was completed.
